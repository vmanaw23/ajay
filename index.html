Born in 1969 in Chakradharpur, a small mining town in Jharkhand, Started from Delhi and in 1999, he moved to Bombay and established himself as a practicing artist. From the beginning of his artistic journey the Chotanagpur Plateau, its mines, fields and the hillocks of minerals inspire him to produce abstracted and mind-born forms of his native land.
